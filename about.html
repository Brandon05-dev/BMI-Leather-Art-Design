About Us

About Us

BMI Leather Art and Design is a celebration of handcrafted excellence, where every product reflects the skill and passion of our artisans. Founded on values of authenticity, creativity, and sustainability, we specialize in custom leatherwork that tells a story.

Our Mission

At BMI Leather Art and Design, our mission is to blend Kenyan tradition with modern craftsmanship by producing high-quality, handcrafted leather products that promote sustainability and empower local artisans.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized brand that showcases the beauty of African craftsmanship while championing ethical production and community empowerment.

Join Our Journey

Whether you're looking for timeless leather pieces, want to support Kenyan artisans, or you're simply passionate about sustainable fashion you're in the right place.
