Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, processes, and policies.

Custom projects typically take 2-4 weeks depending on complexity. Simple accessories may be completed in 1-2 weeks, while complex items like briefcases or custom bags may take 4-6 weeks. We'll provide you with a detailed timeline during your consultation.
We work with a variety of high-quality leathers including full-grain cowhide, vegetable-tanned leather, soft nappa, and exotic leathers. All our materials are ethically sourced and carefully selected for durability and beauty.
Yes, we offer warranties on all our services. Care services include a 30-day guarantee, repairs come with a 90-day warranty, and custom creations are backed by our lifetime craftsmanship guarantee against defects.
In most cases, yes. Our skilled craftsmen can match colors and textures very closely, though exact matches depend on the age and type of the original leather. We'll assess your item and discuss options during consultation.
We offer pickup and delivery services within Nairobi and surrounding areas for an additional fee. For customers outside our service area, we can arrange secure shipping with full insurance coverage.
We accept cash, mobile money (M-Pesa), bank transfers, and major credit cards. For custom projects, we typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the balance due upon completion.
