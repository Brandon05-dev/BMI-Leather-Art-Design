Our Story

Crafting Tradition, Inspiring Innovation

BMI Leather Art & Design was born from a passion for preserving traditional leather craftsmanship while embracing contemporary design sensibilities. Our workshop in Kenya serves as a creative hub where skilled artisans transform premium leather into exceptional pieces that stand the test of time.

Each creation is a testament to our commitment to quality, sustainability, and artistic excellence. We believe that true luxury lies not just in the finished product, but in the story it tells and the values it represents.
