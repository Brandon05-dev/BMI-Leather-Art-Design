Our Story

Where tradition meets innovation, and every stitch tells a story of passion, craftsmanship, and unwavering commitment to excellence.

Crafting Excellence Since 2010

BMI Leather Art & Design was born from a simple yet profound belief: that in our increasingly digital world, there's something irreplaceably valuable about objects made by human hands with care, skill, and attention to detail.

Our journey began in the vibrant workshops of Nairobi, Kenya, where traditional leather crafting techniques have been passed down through generations. We recognized the opportunity to preserve these time-honored methods while creating contemporary designs that speak to modern sensibilities.

"Every piece we create is not just a product, but a testament to the enduring power of human creativity and the timeless appeal of authentic craftsmanship."

How We Work

Our Crafting Process

From concept to completion, every step in our process is carefully executed to ensure exceptional results.

1

Design & Consultation

We begin with understanding your vision, discussing requirements, and creating detailed design sketches that capture your unique style and functional needs.

2

Material Selection

Only the finest, ethically-sourced leather is chosen for each project. We carefully select materials based on the intended use, aesthetic preferences, and durability requirements.

3

Pattern Making

Precise patterns are created and tested to ensure perfect fit and function. This critical step determines the final proportions and structural integrity of the piece.

4

Cutting & Preparation

Each piece is carefully cut by hand, with attention to grain direction and hide characteristics. This ensures optimal strength and beautiful aging over time.

5

Assembly & Stitching

Master craftsmen assemble and stitch each piece using traditional techniques and premium threads, ensuring both beauty and longevity in every seam.

6

Finishing & Quality Control

Final finishing touches are applied, including edge work, conditioning, and detailed quality inspection to ensure each piece meets our exacting standards.

Our Commitment

Sustainable Craftsmanship

We believe that true luxury comes from creating beautiful objects that don't compromise our planet's future. Our commitment to sustainability is woven into every aspect of our business.

  • Ethically Sourced Materials
    All leather is sourced from suppliers who adhere to strict environmental and ethical standards.
  • Water Conservation
    We use eco-friendly tanning processes that minimize water usage and eliminate harmful chemicals.
  • Zero Waste Philosophy
    Leather scraps are repurposed into smaller accessories, ensuring minimal material waste.
  • Community Support
    We support local communities by providing fair wages and skills training opportunities.
