Leather Care Instructions

Avoid Water Damage

Keep leather away from excessive moisture. If wet, allow to air dry naturally.

Protect from Sun

Avoid prolonged exposure to direct sunlight to prevent fading and cracking.

Regular Cleaning

Use a soft, dry cloth for regular cleaning. Apply leather conditioner monthly.

Proper Storage

Store in a cool, dry place with good air circulation. Use dust bags when not in use.
